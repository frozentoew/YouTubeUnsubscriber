Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how YouTube Unsubscriber ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information.

Information We Access

When you sign in with your Google account, the app accesses your YouTube subscription list solely to perform the unsubscribe actions you request. We access only the minimum data required to provide this service.

Information We Do NOT Store

  • We do not store your Google password
  • We do not store your YouTube subscription list on our servers
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes

Authentication

Sign-in is handled entirely through Google's official OAuth 2.0 system. We never see or store your Google password. Your session token is used only to communicate with YouTube's official API on your behalf and is not retained after your session ends.

Third Party Services

This app uses the following third party services:

  • Google Sign-In (OAuth 2.0)
  • YouTube Data API v3

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
