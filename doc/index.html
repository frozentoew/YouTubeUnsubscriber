Frequently Asked Questions

Will this delete my Google account?
No. The app only manages your YouTube channel subscriptions. Nothing else is affected.

Is my data safe?
Yes. We use Google's official sign-in and never store your password or credentials.

Can I undo an unsubscribe?
No. Once unsubscribed, you would need to manually re-subscribe to any channels removed by mistake.

How do I revoke the app's access?
Go to Google Account → Security → Third-party apps → find YouTube Unsubscriber → Remove Access.
