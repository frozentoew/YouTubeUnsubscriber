# YouTube Unsubscriber

**Clean up your YouTube subscriptions in seconds.**

YouTube Unsubscriber is an iOS app that lets you bulk unsubscribe from YouTube channels — saving you hours compared to unsubscribing one channel at a time manually.

---

## What It Does

YouTube makes it tedious to unsubscribe from channels — you have to visit each one individually and tap Unsubscribe. YouTube Unsubscriber fixes that by letting you unsubscribe from multiple channels at once, in just a few taps.

---

## Requirements

- iPhone running iOS 13.0 or later
- A Google / YouTube account
- An active internet connection

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Download the App
Download YouTube Unsubscriber from the App Store and open it on your iPhone.

### 2. Sign In with Google
Tap **Sign in with Google** and log in with the Google account connected to your YouTube subscriptions. The app will ask for permission to access your YouTube data — this is required to view and manage your subscriptions.

### 3. View Your Subscriptions
Once signed in, the app will load all the channels you are currently subscribed to.

### 4. Select Channels to Unsubscribe
Tap the channels you want to unsubscribe from. You can select as many as you like.

### 5. Bulk Unsubscribe
Tap the **Unsubscribe** button. The app will remove all selected subscriptions automatically — no need to visit each channel individually.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is this app safe to use?**
Yes. The app uses Google's official OAuth login — we never see or store your Google password. You can revoke the app's access at any time from your Google Account settings.

**Will YouTube know I used this app?**
The unsubscribes are performed through YouTube's official API, so they appear the same as if you unsubscribed manually.

**Can I undo an unsubscribe?**
No — once you unsubscribe from a channel, it cannot be undone automatically. You would need to manually re-subscribe to any channels you removed by mistake. We recommend reviewing your selection carefully before tapping Unsubscribe.

**How many channels can I unsubscribe from at once?**
You can select and unsubscribe from multiple channels in one go. Very large numbers of unsubscribes may take a few moments to process.

**The app is not loading my subscriptions. What should I do?**
Make sure you have an active internet connection, then try signing out and signing back in. If the issue persists, please contact us using the link below.

**How do I revoke the app's access to my YouTube account?**
Go to your Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access → find YouTube Unsubscriber → tap Remove Access.
